Find below a simple recipe for making a delicious pasta dish using common kitchen ingredients.
## Ingredients:
- 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
- 1 cup tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil 
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions:
1. **Cook the Pasta**: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. 
2. **Prepare the Sauce**: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
3. **Combine Ingredients**: Add the tomato sauce to the skillet with the garlic. Stir in the dried oregano and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. **Mix Everything Together**: Pour the sauce over the cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
5. **Serve**: Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
